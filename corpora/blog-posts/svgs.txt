Last year in April, I began to experiment with scalable vector graphics (SVGs).
I posted some of my creations in THL here
[http://www.thehomeworklife.co.nf/index.html?post=73] and here
[http://www.thehomeworklife.co.nf/index.html?post=75].

I just wanted to post a few more here from recent experimentation. All are
vectorized versions (using the scan bitmap to path tool) of existing graphics
created with Inkscape. Most are grabbed off the web, but some are from my own
camera. Enjoy!
